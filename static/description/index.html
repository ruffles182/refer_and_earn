Odoo Refer And Earn Extension

Odoo Website Refer And Earn allows your customers to refer your business to their friends and earn Bonus Points. The referred connections can join using the generated referral code. Hence, the new users will be rewarded that will compel them to purchase from your website.

Referral marketing allows your customers to recommend your brand to their friends and family. It helps you to grow the reach of your business widely as you get the sales from the connections of your customers. Moreover, when a trusted source refers to your business then it increases their chance to make the purchase.

However, your customers need a reason to refer your business; as without any profit there are really less chances for them to refer your business. Hence, you can offer them some rewards for each referral which will bring excitement to market your business in effective way.

Now, let your customers refer your Odoo store to their connection. Odoo Website Refer And Earn facilitates your clients to refer your business to their connections and earn Bonus Points. You can keep a track on the referrals and the reward earned in the real time via personalized dashboard.

Manage the Refer and Earn Policy in a hassle-free way

Moreover, there is an auto-generated Personalised dashboard for your customer to track their reward stats easily in Odoo.
